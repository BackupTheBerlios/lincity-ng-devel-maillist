I'd propose to increase the base cost and lower the multiplier for solar and wind energy. In lower tech-levels the price would be similar to what it is now, but it would not raise so much for higher tech-levels. 
Additionally I would propose a change in the formula to calculate the power production. It could start very low (lower than current production) but raise fast with increasing tech-level ( fast improvement while a technology is still new). Later the growth could slow down as the technology approaches a physical limit ( e.g. 100% efficiency).
Wind power could become cheaper than coal (maybe at tech-level 100) and solar power could eventually become cheaper than wind(around tech-level 200). That would also make high tech levels more attractive.
