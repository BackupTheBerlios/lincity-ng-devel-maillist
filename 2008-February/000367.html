Another tiny remark, to prevent potential mistake. Currently, one can
identify buildings at their "look":
-low birth + high death (50/100): these are "ugly" building (like cemetary ;)
-high birth + low death (100/200): pretty nice "rich" houses
-high birth + high death (200/400): rather "normal" towers

Of course, in the code, the hight tech is not just twice the low tech version!
Having a balance in buildings is needed to control population growth, which is
needed for sutainable way.
