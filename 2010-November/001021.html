On 11/01/2010 10:49 AM, Wolfgang Becker wrote:
> Does it have to be a distribution specific host? I don't like the
> idea of a preferred distribution to play Lincity. But in the end
> it is just a name. The "inactivity" clause in their TOS might be

We only have that clause so that we can reserve the right to clean up
inactive projects if we ever find ourselves desperately in need of disk
space. We're very very far away from that though, so I don't think it
needs to be a practical concern. :)

Also, if the naming is a concern, I think we could come to an
arrangement where Fedora would provide the hosting under a separate
domain name, as long we were given due credit (e.g. something like
"Lincity, a Fedora Hosted Project" on the website footer).
