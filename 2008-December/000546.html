Summary: Ore mine's bulldozing

Follow-Ups:

Date: 2008-Dec-09 21:42
By: jaky_lincity

Comment:
There is a bug that prevents the bulldozing of an oremine until the ore reserve of one of the underlying mappoints is below 50%. The total ore reserve would be at 96.875% at this point. 
In the original lincity and in older lincity-ng versions you could always bulldoze and only the mappoints with less than 50% ore reserve would turn into water.
The patch at http://developer.berlios.de/patch/index.php?func=detailpatch&patch_id=2607&group_id=2929 restores the old behaviour. Maybe we could also add a warning message to ask the user if he really wants to bulldoze.
