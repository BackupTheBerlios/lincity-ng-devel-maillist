On 2009-01-17 Ivar ten Cate <varivar at gmail.com> wrote:
> <image file="waterlrd.png" x="64" y="28" />
> The x-coordinate works, but the y-coordinate doesn't. Did I do
> something wrong or is it impossible to change the y-coordinate?

It is not possible. The y-coordinate generated by contrib/autocrop
is wrong anyway (see data/images/tiles/README.txt). It is ignored
by the renderer.  Every image has to cover the entire area of the
tile, it is allowed to draw above the baseline, but not below. So
the bottom of the image is alway the bottom of the tile.
