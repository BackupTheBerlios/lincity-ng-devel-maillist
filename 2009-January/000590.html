On Tue, Jan 06, 2009 at 08:57:42PM +0100, Ivar ten Cate wrote:
> 
> Btw, how do you make scenarios? I tried to make a scenario with
> islands in an ocean, but I didn't manage to add trees and to
> add/remove desert tiles. I assume there's a special trick to do this.

AFAIK scenarios are just normal savegames. To turn desert into trees or 
bare land the FLAG_HAS_UNDERGROUND_WATER has to be changed for the tile.
As this is difficult to do directly in the savegame I wrote a function 
which changed some random desert tiles to bare land when the user presses F6.
