By: cman79

Comment:
This behavior arises from an inconsistency with paragraphs spans and styles. Every span can have its individual style, so effectively they are nothing else than alternate paragraphs. So what happens is that the longer email address is rendered
on top of the shorter names. This behavior is quite handy for rendering left and right adjust columns with fixed margins as in mps display. So for now the simplest workaround should be to make distinct paragraphs for names and emails.
