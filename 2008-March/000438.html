Hi,

I've finished the road bridge and the track bridge, both are in SVN now.
I'll explain the parts of the bridges below.

The track bridge:
Old looking stone bridge.
Trackbridge1.png and 2: straight bridge part.
Trackbridge_entrance_xyz.png: approach ramps. The numbers (00, 90, 180 and
270) represents the amount of rotation (degrees)

The road bridge:
Modern concrete bridge. The approach ramp exists of two tiles. Both are at
ground tiles.
Roadbridge1.png and 2: straight bridge part.
Roadbridge1s.png and 2: straight bridge part with support (for long bridges)
Roadbridge_entrance1_xyz.png: ramp part 1
Roadbridge_entrance2_xyz.png: ramp part 2

I added two examples to SVN (
http://svn.berlios.de/viewcvs/lincity-ng/trunk/data/models/):
Track bridge: Trackbridge-example.png
Road bridge: Bridge_example.png

know nothing of programming:(
