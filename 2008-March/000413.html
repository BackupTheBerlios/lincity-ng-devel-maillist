I have also one thing which can be added to the game: water irrigation in
deserts. Water irrigation
can be a good way for the user to get rid of deserts. I think it should be
something like this:
the user can build a water irrigation system (which I can draw :) ) on a
desert tile. Of course
a water irrigation system costs money and it should be placed near a water
well or river. After some
months, the desert tile turns in a grass tile and the water system goes away
(automatically).
In that way a user can turn entire deserts in lucrative ground, on which he
can build parks, farms
and other stuff.
