It will be for 2.+
I have tons of ideas like this, as my university studies were about
modeling in oceanography and climate, but Lincity must stay a nice
simple and clever game :)
Implementing a climate model is too difficult and would eat CPU and memory.

I think we don't need an additional parameter like ozone, as we already
have air pollution.
My initial idea for doing waterwell (and it is still my aim) was just adding
effect of pollution that "kill" land and make deserts, and/or pollute water
with the same consequences (hence the need for altitude to know were the
water goes, and this part is not yet finished).

Doing more subtle pollution needs more work, and some time to experiment
and tune to keep playability... so it will be later.
