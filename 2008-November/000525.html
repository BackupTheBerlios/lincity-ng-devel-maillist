Summary: Empty ore mine doesn't get converted to water

Details: Hi,

Using 1.92beta some empty ore mines don't turn into water.

It seems that the ones that don't touch navegable water get converted as soon as they're empty



Follow-Ups:

Date: 2008-Nov-04 01:51
By: none

Comment:
I think the problem is that the check if the mine is empty takes place inside the animation phase. If it is not time for an animation when the mine stops operating it doesn't turn into water. After the mine stops operating the animation also stops, therefor the deletion of the mine never takes place. I wrote a patch that moves the check out of the animation-if-block of oremine.cpp
I submitted the patch at https://developer.berlios.de/patch/index.php?func=detailpatch&patch_id=2584&group_id=2929 , because I couldn't find a way to add the patch to this bug.
