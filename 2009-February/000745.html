Hello,

In the discussion about pollution in Bug #15177 several people said that the light industry pollution is too high. As I said in this bug report I propose to reduce the pollution from about 90/day to 1.8/day. I attached a patch to do that.

There seems to be a bug in the way the market burns waste. ATM it only burns waste if it is connected to a track. The second patch I attached fixes that. It also reduces the amount of waste burned from 70% of the market capacity to 10% of the market capacity. That way it is easier to see for the player that their markets are full of waste. The pollution/waste is also reduced slightly so that it is the same as when burning waste on the transport.
What do the other developers think about it ? Should I commit these patches ?

Jaky
