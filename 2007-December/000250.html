Hi,

just played a bit with latest svn version. Is it just me or is the
startup (start game until you see the menu) slower? No idea why that
should happen.

Same with starting a new game. But that might be caused by a bit more
complex terrain generator. Or I'm just imagining it was faster.

Anyway the landscape looks much nicer with the forest. The random
ponds make gameplay a bit harder because you can't build as room
efficient which makes the city look more realistic. The lack of roads in
the start village is a problem. You get to keep the ponds there, too.
So it is impossible to build the road any time soon. Don't the communes
need roads any more? Even if the don't I'd prefer to have roads in the
random village instead of water that blocks further building.

As far as I can tell, current svn is good enough to build a beta
release. That way more people can test if the missing roads really make
a difference.
