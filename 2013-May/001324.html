Multiplayer mode sounds nice, but is definitely a big chunk of work. Parts
of the codebase are quite old (17 years?), so there could possibly be a
need for more complete rewrite of the game.

I personally don't know a much about the codebase. Basically it's divided
into two parts: old lincity and lincity-ng. (At first Lincity-ng was just
new graphics to old game engine, I think). IIRC the old part is more C and
the new part is C++ – thus object oriented. Nowadays there are some
additions to the game engine, but I'm not sure if they are implemented to
old code or into new code.Anyway, don't take my words too literally: I
don't really know much about this game's codebase.
