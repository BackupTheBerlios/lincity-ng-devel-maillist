Hello,

I wrote this sentence after adding the feature in the game.
"flow" is how much traffic (0-100%) there is for specific commodiy on 
any transport tile
"level" is how much quantity (0-100%) there is of a specific commodity 
in a place.
A flow always requires a tiny difference in levels. just like a river does.

I hope that answers your question.
