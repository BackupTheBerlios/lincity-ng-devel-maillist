>  *General comments :*

* Money *
Nuclear plants are very expensive to build (billion euros)

*  Client to buy production *
Nuclear plant produces GW, and linicty town is too small to reach such need.
One nuclear plant would probably be too much for the town.

* time scale *
Buildings does not get old in lincity, they last forever, but this is not a 
problem (we can consider that they are rebuilded regurlarly, as in Real Life)

This *must not*  be the case for nuclear plants, which lasts near 50 years, 
and then need to be retired, which causes the problem of nuclears wastes and 
contaminated material management (uranium and tons of concrete). 

If this is a real pain in the game (forbidding some part of the land for long 
time, cost a huge amount of money, and sometimes causes very bad hazard)
then i agree that nuclear plant can be integrated in the game, and will 
improve the clever educational aspect of the game about a very actual hot 
topic ;-)  (in france we have lots of nuclear plants that will be retired 
soon, currently the only choice was to increase their lifetime by 10 or 20 
years, and do nothing else !)

If the nuclear plants are eternal, i'm strongly opposite to implementing them, 
at it would hide the main problem or wastes management.
