Hello.

I compared r1275 and r1276 (first version with water well and
forest/desert zones(tiles) on the map)

It seems to me that such an abundance of trees and deserts in random
order spoils the overall look of the game.

In the real world allocation is more grouped. Trees cannot be in close
proximity to the desert.
Desert cannot be in close proximity to the river or water.

I propose two solutions:
* Change the tree texture, maybe trees have to be smaller. Trees are
merging with buildings now.
* Rewrite map generator. For example:
** a water tile don't have any desert around 3-7 tiles.
** a tree tile don't have any desert around 3-7 tiles.
** forests instead of trees. For example make a random tree tile line
(3-8 tiles). Make random tree tiles around each tree tile of the tree
tile line.
** Create random desert tile a similar manner.

What do you think about it?
