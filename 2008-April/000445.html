Hi to all,

Here is the first patch to manage track and road bridges.
Bridges are build by using track/road dragging on the water.

For a road or track section between 2 bridge sections (and entrances), 
we need a pillar bridge section with green; for the moment I have 
toggled it to bridge (without over cost).

If you bulldoze a bridge section, the result is a desert (like water).
The performance seems to be correct but I have some ideas to enhance it.
Upgrade transport works with bridge too.

Comments and testers are welcome :)
